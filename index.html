DIY: Build & Test

  1. Cut copper/steel foils with factory edges; pair to form a slit.
  2. Shine a safe laser through the slit; capture the image on card.
  3. Flip the slit; compare left/right asymmetry. Photograph both.
  4. Record distance, laser type; share images and notes.

Safety first: low-power laser + eye protection.
